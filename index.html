our story

A dhaba built on flame, spice and soul

Papa Päncho da Dhaba brings the warmth of a Punjabi roadside eatery to Bandra. Expect hearty curries, charred kebabs and breads that are soft, buttery and fragrant.

Every dish is made to share — from slow-simmered dal makhani to tandoor-fresh paneer tikka and thick, creamy lassi.
